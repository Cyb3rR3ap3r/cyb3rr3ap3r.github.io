whoami

Hi, my name is Trevor Isenberg aka Cyb3rR3ap3r. I am currently an IT professional looking to move into the CyberSecurity field. I want to give back to the InfoSec community so I decided to start this blog website that will host various CTF walkthroughs as well as other interesting blog posts related to InfoSec.

What makes these CTF Walkthroughs different is it will not only walk you through the path to get root but will also include a sample pentest report detailing potenital other mis configurations that would be reported if the machine was on a real network.

I decided to do this as a way to work on my report writing skills as well as training my methodology to look at all potential mis configurations instead of finding one path and stopping. I hope you find this useful and please feel free to reach out on Twitter/Linkedin or check out my GitHub for badly programming projects I have worked on (All linked at bottom of page)
